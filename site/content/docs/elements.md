## Titles

There are 6 levels of titles supported:

```
 - # Title 1
 - ## Title 2
```

They'll produce this:

# Title 1
## Title 2
### Title 3
#### Title 4
##### Title 5
###### Title 6

## Images

You can import images:

```
![Image alt/description](photo.jpg)
```
